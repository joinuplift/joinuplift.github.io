UPLIFT FOR IMPLEMENTERS

Welcome! Your work as an implementer and champion of the Graduation approach is already advancing efforts to end extreme poverty globally. Uplift is here to help you achieve this goal, connecting you to a community of other implementers and the resources you need to reach more people and achieve results at scale. Working together, we can achieve more than ever before to break the cycle of poverty and create opportunities for those who need it most.

Here, you’ll find a growing resource library with presentations and other documents to support your work. This is just the beginning. Over time, this site will expand to serve as a collaborative platform for sharing best practices, learning materials and other resources to strengthen and expand Graduation programs globally.
